Extended Strengths and Weaknesses Assessment of Normal Behavior (E-SWAN)¶

The Extended Strengths and Weaknesses Assessment of Normal Behavior (E-SWAN) is a parent self-report instrument used to help assess multiple DSM Disorders and evaluate problem behavior in children and adolescents.  The disorders include Depression, Disruptive Mood Disregulation Disorder (DMDD), Social Anxiety, and Panic Disorder.

Domains Assessed: Anxiety, Depression and Mood, Psychotic and Atypical Behavior

Note: The E-SWAN is administered to parents of participants of children ages 5-17.

References: Developed by the Child Mind Institute

Time to Complete: 10 minutes
