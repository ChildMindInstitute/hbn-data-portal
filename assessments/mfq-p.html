Mood and Feelings Questionnaire – Long Version Parent Report (MFQ-P)¶

The MFQ consists of a series of descriptive phrases regarding how the subject has been feeling or acting recently. Codings reflect whether the phrase was descriptive of the subject most of the time, sometimes, or not at all in the past two weeks.

Domains Assessed: Depression and Mood

Note: The MFQ-P is administered to parents of participants ages 8-17.

References: Angold, A., Costello, E. J., Messer, S. C., Pickles, A., Winder, F., & Silver, D. (1995) The development of a short questionnaire for use in epidemiological studies of depression in children and adolescents. International Journal of Methods in Psychiatric Research, 5, 237 - 249.

Time to Complete: 10 minutes
