Beck Depression Inventory - II (BDI)¶

The BDI is no longer administered to participants

The BDI-II is a 21-question multiple-choice self-report inventory, one of the most widely used instruments for measuring the severity of depression.

Domains Assessed: Depression and Mood

Note: The BDI is administered to participants ages 18+.

References:  Beck AT, Steer RA, Garbin MG J (1988). "Psychometric properties of the Beck Depression Inventory Twenty-five years of evaluation". Clinical Psychology Reviews, 8, 77–100.

Time to Complete: 10 minutes
