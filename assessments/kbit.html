All participants under age 6 years are administered the KBIT-II as a brief measure of verbal and non-verbal intellectual function and ability.

Note: The KBIT is administered to participants of age 5 or with limited intellectual abilities.

References: Kaufman, A. S., & Kaufman, N. S. (2004). Kaufman Brief Intelligence Test -II. Bloomington, MN: Pearson.

Time to Complete: 20 minutes
