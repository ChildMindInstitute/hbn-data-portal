Inventory of Callous-Unemotional Traits – Parent Report (ICUT)¶

The Inventory of Callous-Unemotional Traits (ICUT) is a copyright protected 24-item questionnaire designed to provide a comprehensive assessment of callous and unemotional traits.  These traits have proven to be important for designating a distinct subgroup group of antisocial and aggressive youth. The ICUT has three subscales: Callousness, Uncaring, and Unemotional.

Domains Assessed: Personality Traits

Note: The ICUT is administered to parents of all participants.

References: Essau, C.A., Sasagawa, S., & Frick, P.J. (2006). Callous-unemotional traits in a community sample of adolescents. Assessment, 132, 454-469.

Time to Complete: 10-15 minutes
