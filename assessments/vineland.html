Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scale – Parent/ Caregiver Rating Form¶

The Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scales is a measure of adaptive behavior from birth to adulthood. The Vineland forms an aid in diagnosing and classifying intellectual and developmental disabilities.

Domains Assessed: Psychotic and Atypical Behavior

Note: The Vineland is administered to parents of participants with developmental or intellectual disorders.

References: Sparrow, S., Cicchetti, D., & Balla, D. (2005) Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scales, Second Edition.

Time to Complete: 1 hour
