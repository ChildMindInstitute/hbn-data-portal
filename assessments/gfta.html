Goldman-Fristoe Test of Articulation - III (GFTA)¶

The GFTA provides information about a child’s articulation ability by sampling both spontaneous and imitative sound production. Use this test to measure articulation of consonant sounds, determine types of misarticulation, and compare individual performance to national, gender-differentiated norms.

Domains Assessed: Verbal Learning

Note: Part 1 of the GFTA (Sounds-in-words) is administered to all participants. Part 2 (Sounds-in-sentences) is administered to participants receiving an additional speech/language evaluation.

References: Goldman, R. and Fristoe, M. (2015) Goldman-Fristoe Test of Articulation – Third Edition (GFTA-3). Circle Pines, MN: American Guidance Service, Inc.

Time to Complete: 15 minutes
