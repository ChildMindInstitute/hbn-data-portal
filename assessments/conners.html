Conners ADHD Rating Scales - Self Report Short Form¶

The Conners ADHD Rating Scale is an instrument that uses self-report ratings to help assess attention deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and evaluate problem behavior in children and adolescents.

Domains Assessed: Aggression and Oppositionality, Attention Problems and Hyperactivity, Cognitive and Executive Function, Social Interaction

Note: The Conners is administered to partipants ages 11-17.

References: Conners, C.K. (2001). Conners’ Rating Scales-Revised. North Tonawanda, NY: Multi-Health Systems, Inc.

Time to Complete: 10 minutes
