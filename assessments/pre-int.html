A set of pre-interview questionnaires are administered to parents/caregivers of participants. Data collected from the questionnaires are grouped into the following categories:

  • Family History/Research Diagnostic Criteria
  • Demographics/Family (Questions correspond to information about family members)
  • Demographic and Household Information (Questions correspond to participant's living situation)
  • Developmental History
  • Education and Social History
  • Family Medical/Psychiatric History
  • Language

Domains Assessed: Background Information

Note: The pre-interview questionnaires are administered to parents of all participants.

References: Designed by the Healthy Brain Network

Time to Complete: 60-75 minutes
