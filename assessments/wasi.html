The Wechsler Abbreviated Scale of Intelligence provides a full scale intelligence quotient (FSIQ), verbal IQ (VIQ), and performance IQ (PIQ) for ages 6-89 years. The Vocabulary, Similarities, Block Design, and Matrix Reasoning subtests will be used to estimate Full Scale IQ. This test can be easily administered by any study team member following brief training. Training ise provided by an experience clinician on the research team.

Domains Assessed: Cognitive and Executive Functioning, Verbal Learning

Note: The WASI is administered to participants ages 17+.

References: Wechsler, D. (1999). Wechsler Abbreviated Scale of Intelligence. San Antonio, TX: The Psychological Corporation.

Time to Complete: 30 minutes
