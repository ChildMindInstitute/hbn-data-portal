The Dishion Social Acceptance Scale assess youths' peer associates and acceptance (i.e., liking) and rejection (i.e., disliking) by peers.

Domains Assessed: Social Interaction

Note: The Dishion Social Acceptance Scale is administered to teachers of all school-age participants.

References: Dishion, T. J., Kim, H., Stormshak, E. A., & O'Neill, M. (2014). A brief measure of peer affiliation and social acceptance (PASA): Validity in an ethnically diverse sample of early adolescents. Journal of Clinical Child & Adolescent Psychology, 43(4), 601-612.

Time to Complete: 5-10 minutes
