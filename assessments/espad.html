European School Survey Project on Alcohol and Other Drugs (ESPAD)¶

The ESPAD is an adaptive questionnaire. It consists of a 16-question drug and alcohol screening form and additional follow up questions only for substances that a participant endorses.

Domains Assessed: Substance Use and Addictive Behavior

Note: The DTS is administered to participants ages 10+.

References: Hibell, B., Guttormsson, U., Ahlström, S., et al. (2012), The 2011 ESPAD Report: substance use among students in 36 European countries. The Swedish Council for Information on Alcohol and Other Drugs (CAN), Stockholm, Sweden.

Time to Complete: 15 minutes
