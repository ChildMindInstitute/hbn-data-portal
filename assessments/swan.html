The SWAN ADHD Rating Scale is an instrument that uses observer ratings to help assess attention deficit/hyperactivity disorder (ADHD) and evaluate problem behavior in children and adolescents.

Domains Assessed: Attention Problems and Hyperactivity, Cognitive and Executive Functioning

Note: The SWAN is administered to parents of participants ages 6-17.

References: Swanson, J., Schuck, S., Mann, M., Carlson, C., Hartman, K., Sergeant, J., . . . McCleary, R. (2001). The SWAN rating scale.
