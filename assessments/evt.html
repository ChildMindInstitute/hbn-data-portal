Expressive Vocabulary Test, Second Edition (EVT-2)¶

The Expressive Vocabulary Test (EVT) is an individually administered, norm-referenced instrument that assesses expressive vocabulary and word retrieval for children and adults. The EVT-2 measures expressive vocabulary knowledge with two types of items: labeling items and synonym items.

Domains Assessed: Verbal Learning

Note: The EVT-2 is administered to all participants who receive an additional speech language evaluation.

References: Williams, K. T. (1997). Expressive Vocabulary Test Second Edition (EVT™ 2). J. Am. Acad. Child Adolesc. Psychiatry, 42, 864-872.

Time to Complete: 15 minutes
