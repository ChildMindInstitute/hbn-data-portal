The AUDIT is a simple, 10-item form developed by the World Health Organization to screen for problem or hazardous alcohol use in a clinical setting.

Domains Assessed: Substance Use and Addictive Behavior

Note: The AUDIT is administered to participants ages 11+.

References: Saunders, J.B., Aasland, O.G., Babor, T.F., de la Fuente, J.R. and Grant, M. (1993). Development of the Alcohol Use Disorders Identification Test (AUDIT): WHO collaborative project on early detection of persons with harmful alcohol consumption. II. Addiction, 88, 791-804.

Time to Complete: 5 minutes
