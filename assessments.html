Disorder-Specific Diagnostic Tools¶

For a subset of psychiatric disorders, specific follow-up assessments are completed. These targeted supplemental diagnostic assessments are not administered to individuals without a suspicion of the presence of clinically significant illness in the corresponding domain, due to the low likelihood of meaningful variation in most individuals. Data are currently available for speech/language disorder assessments and the Vineland Adaptive Behavior Scale.
